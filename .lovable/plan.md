

# Manish Pure Desi Ghee – Business Website

A professional, clean business website for a desi ghee wholesale supplier with warm, inviting colors (golds, creams, earthy tones) to reflect the traditional and premium nature of the product.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section** – Full-width banner with heading "100% Pure & Authentic Desi Ghee", subheading about traditional methods, and two CTA buttons: "Contact Us" (scrolls/links to contact) and "Wholesale Inquiry" (scrolls to inquiry form)
- **Why Choose Us** – 4-column grid with icons highlighting: Pure & Adulteration Free, Traditional Slow Heating, Rich Aroma & Taste, Bulk Supply Available
- **About Our Ghee** – Text section about purity, tradition, and trust with info about bulk supply specialization
- **Our Vision** – Highlighted section about becoming a trusted name in the desi ghee industry
- **Product Specifications** – Clean card/table showing packaging options (1kg, 5kg, 15kg, 15L tins), shelf life, storage, supply area, and minimum order quantity
- **Wholesale Inquiry Form** – Form with fields: Full Name, Business Name, Phone, Email, City & State, Quantity Required (dropdown: 50–100kg, 100–500kg, 500kg+), and Message. On submission, a success toast is shown (note: actual email sending would require a backend integration later)

### 2. About Page
- Heading and content about the company's commitment to quality, hygiene, purity, and serving wholesalers

### 3. Contact Page
- Contact details displayed with icons: Location (New Delhi, India), Phone (9999999), Email (manishagg.@gmail.com)

### 4. Navigation & Layout
- Sticky header with logo/brand name "Manish Pure Desi Ghee" and nav links: Home, About, Contact
- Footer with business name, quick links, and contact info
- Fully responsive design for mobile and desktop
